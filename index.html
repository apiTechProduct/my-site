About

Most PMs translate between business and engineering. I don't need to.

I grew up in the code. M.S. in Technology from BITS Pilani. First role: full-stack engineer. Then lead engineer. Then architect. I designed systems before I ever wrote a PRD — and that sequence matters more than most people realize.

When I moved into product leadership, I brought all of it with me. The ability to read a pull request. To walk into an architecture review and hold my own. To earn engineering trust because I've been on their side of the table.

Over 14+ years, I've built and led products at the intersection of APIs, developer experience, and platform strategy. At Worldpay, I own APIs, webhooks, and the developer portal end-to-end. I sit on the Enterprise Architecture Review Board. I lead a team of PMs, Technical Writers, and System Analysts. I'm completing MIT's AI Strategy program while actively building with AI tools every single day.

But the outcomes I'm proudest of aren't on a dashboard. They're the engineers who trusted me, the customers whose lives got easier, and the teams that kept shipping long after the roadmap changed.
